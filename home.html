Dripping Tree Farm was founded in 2018. We are located in central Kentucky. We are a family of four - Jim, Elza, Solon, and Anavay - who decided to moved from Salem, Oregon to Liberty, Kentucky in 2018. The farm started with the purchase of four Nubian wethers (Romulus, Remus, D'Artagnan, and Mr. Black). After that, we elected to buy some cows, pigs, goslings, rabbits, guinea, and Spanish goats, but one by one we chose to sell those animals. We are now the host of a several ducks, chickens, cats, and, of course, our growing herd of Nubian goats.
